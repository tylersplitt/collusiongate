The year was 2012. It was a simpler time. Our first season of Fantasy Football and with an elite corps of Quarterbacks taken in the first round this rag tag bunch of high school friends was ready to embark on a Fantasy Football journey that would last for decades. Little did they know, this season would become shrouded in a cloud of controversy as two players gave in to the dark side and chose a path of evil. These are the FACTS of that event.
